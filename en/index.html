湾岸 Midnight

The Midnight bicycle team on the Bay coast was founded on February 22, 2019. With the passage of time, the number of the team has reached 16, and most of the team are high school students, A group of students who are keen to challenge the limit, love cycling, constantly explore and persevere, Constantly improve yourself in riding, break through your limits and challenge the limits you have never challenged. The team members are versatile, not only limited to the love of cycling, but also have different hobbies and skills from others, Even though their studies are heavy, they can't stop these young and energetic teenagers from riding bicycles, Running on mountains, forests and roads.
